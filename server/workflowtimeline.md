# Workflow Timeline & Constraints

## Status Progression
DRAFT → NIGHT_SUBMITTED → MORNING_SUBMITTED → FINALIZED
↓                                                    ↓
└────────────── REOPENED ────────────────────────┘

## What Can Be Done at Each Stage

### DRAFT
✅ Night Entries (create/edit)
✅ Night Collections (create/edit/approve)
✅ Vehicle Allocations (create/edit)  ← MUST complete before NIGHT_SUBMITTED

### NIGHT_SUBMITTED (after submitting night entries)
✅ Morning Entries (create/edit)
✅ Morning Collections (create/edit/approve)
✅ Purchases (create/edit)  ← Only available now (depends on vehicle allocations)
✅ Trays (create/edit)
❌ Vehicle Allocations (edit) ← Locked

### MORNING_SUBMITTED (after submitting morning entries)
✅ Admin Collections (edit/approve)
❌ Purchases (edit) ← Locked
❌ Vehicle Allocations (edit) ← Locked

### FINALIZED
✅ Reopen Paper (only)

### REOPENED
✅ Morning Entries (create/edit)
✅ Purchases (create/edit)
✅ Vehicle Allocations (edit) ← Can re-edit after reopening
Then → FINALIZED
